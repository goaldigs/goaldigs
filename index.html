Why Should you attend this workshop?


Learning about our workshop can help you gain common knowledge about this problem that we are facing. Because of the lives affected by it, it cannot continue to spread nor leave us with no choice. This is why we’re here to promote how important our topic is. Sharing our knowledge with you is as much as gaining knowledge with you.

Here at our workshop, we will guide participants through the essential steps of developing a healthy lifestyle and help them prevent getting micronutrient deficiencies.

It is also designed to engage, educate, and motivate our participants to incorporate wellness into their lives.
